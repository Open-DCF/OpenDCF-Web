A New Era in Financial Modeling

OpenDCF is an open-source, free alternative to Argus Enterprise and Anaplan. Our mission is to democratize institutional-grade financial modeling, making sophisticated discounted cash flow analysis reliable, transparent, and community-owned.

Open Source
Built by the community, for the community. Full transparency and collaboration at every level of development.
Free Forever
No licensing fees, no hidden costs. Enterprise-grade tools accessible to everyone, from students to professionals.
Institutional Quality
Professional-grade financial modeling capabilities that meet the standards of the world's leading institutions.
